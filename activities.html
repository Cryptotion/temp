ACTIVITIES

Capacity Building and Skill Development

Entrepreneurship Development Providing resources and mentorship for budding entrepreneurs through incubators and accelerators.
Technical Skill Enhancement Offering technical training and certifications to improve the skills of MSME employees.

Support for Innovation and Startups

Startup Hubs and Incubators: Facilities and support for new enterprises, including MSMEs. Innovation Labs: Centers that provide resources and support for developing new products and services.

Mentorship Programs

Pairing: Connect new entrepreneurs with experienced mentors who can provide guidance and support. Workshops: Conduct sessions on leadership, business strategy, and other key areas.

Networking and Trade Shows

Industry Events and Trade Fairs: Opportunities for MSMEs to showcase their products and network with potential partners.
Business Forums and Conferences: Platforms for MSMEs to discuss challenges, share knowledge, and explore new opportunities.

Support for Rural and Underserved Areas

Rural MSME Development Programs: Initiatives aimed at promoting and supporting MSMEs in rural and remote areas.
Inclusive Growth Strategies: Programs designed to include underserved communities and regions in the MSME ecosystem.

Financial Support and Credit Facilities

Loan Facilitation
Providing financial advisory services, helping MSMEs access loans, manage working capital, and create financial strategies.
SIDBI Assistance The Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) offers various financial products and services to MSMEs.

Social Impact and Inclusion

Support for Women and Marginalized Groups: Offering targeted support for women entrepreneurs and marginalized communities through specific schemes and training programs.

Research and Policy Advocacy

Policy Research: Studies and reports on MSME challenges and opportunities to inform policy decisions.
